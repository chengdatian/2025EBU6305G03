❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from our users. Click on a question to expand the answer.

How is my learning progress saved?

All progress is automatically saved to your browser's local storage — no account, login, or internet required! Your completed modules, quiz scores, and game statistics are all stored locally on your device and are 100% private to you.

How can I improve my test and game scores?

Master all Learning Modules first, as all quiz and game questions are based on the official curriculum. Practice with the Interactive Games to build muscle memory for color codes, and review incorrect answers in the Test Module's Wrong Review section. The Practice Mode in the Test Module is perfect for targeted learning of specific color models.

Can I use this platform offline?

YES! The entire platform runs locally in your browser. No internet connection is needed after the initial page load. You can save the page to your device for full offline access, perfect for learning on the go.

How do I reset all my progress and data?

You can reset individual module progress directly in each module: go to the Game/Test/Learning modules and click the "Reset Progress" button. To reset all data at once, you can clear your browser's local storage for this site, or use the "Reset All Settings" button in the Accessibility tab.

Are new questions and content added regularly?

Yes! We update quiz questions, game challenges, and learning content weekly. New color models, advanced topics, and game modes are added regularly based on user feedback.

What browsers and devices are supported?

All modern browsers are fully supported: Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari (latest 2 versions). The platform is fully responsive and works on all devices: mobile phones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. For the best experience, we recommend using Chrome or Edge on a desktop device.

Is this platform suitable for complete beginners?

Absolutely! We start with basic color theory and progress to advanced coding concepts. The Learning Module is designed for complete beginners, with interactive demos and step-by-step explanations. The games and tests have multiple difficulty levels, so you can learn at your own pace.

Do I need any prior design or coding experience?

No prior experience is required at all! This platform is designed to teach you everything you need to know about color coding from scratch. Whether you're a student, designer, developer, or just curious about color theory, this platform is for you.

How do I get my color coding certificate?

You can earn your official Color Coding Certification by completing the Formal Quiz in the Test Module with a score of 80% or higher. Once you pass, you can generate and download a high-resolution certificate directly from the results page.

Is my data private and secure?

100% private and secure! We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data on external servers. All your progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device only. We do not track your activity, collect analytics, or use cookies for advertising purposes.

Common Issues & Solutions

Page not loading correctly or showing blank screen
  1. Refresh the page using F5 or the refresh button in your browser
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies for this site
  3. Update your browser to the latest version
  4. Disable any ad blockers or script blockers that may be interfering
  5. Try using a different browser (Chrome or Edge recommended)
Games or interactive elements not working
  1. Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser settings
  2. Update your browser to the latest version (older browsers may not support modern JavaScript features)
  3. Refresh the page to reload all scripts
  4. Disable any browser extensions that may be blocking JavaScript
  5. For mobile users: try rotating to landscape mode for better usability
Learning progress or settings not saving
  1. Ensure local storage is enabled in your browser settings
  2. Make sure you are not using incognito/private browsing mode (local storage is cleared when the window is closed)
  3. Do not clear your browser cache/cookies for this site, as this will delete all your saved data
  4. Refresh the page and check if your progress reappears
Display glitches or styling issues
  1. Refresh the page to reload all CSS styles
  2. Update your browser to the latest version
  3. Disable any custom themes or browser extensions that modify page styling
  4. Reset your accessibility settings if you have made changes that affect display
  5. Use Chrome or Edge for the best display compatibility
Audio not working
  1. Ensure audio is enabled in the navigation bar (volume icon is not muted)
  2. Check your device volume and ensure it is not muted
  3. Ensure your browser has permission to play audio
  4. Refresh the page and try again
  5. Note: All functionality is fully usable without audio
